Bear

This is the grizzly bear designed by Queintin Trollip. Diagrams can be found in the book "Origami sequence". I used a 35*35cm paper but 50*50cm or more would be better.

Dinosour (T-Rex)

This is the T-Rex designed by Kade Chan. I used a printer paper to fold this model.

Elephant

This is the sevillia elephant designed by Joseph Wu. He highly recomends kami paper, but I used printer paper which is not that great for this model.

Fawn

This fawn is designed by Stephen Weiss. It is a very elegant model of intermediate difficulty. I used 20*20cm sheet of printer paper.

Hedge hog

This hedgehog is deigned by Paulius Mielinis. I used 30*30 cm copper tissue foil paper.

Tree Frogs

The tree frog, designed by Robert Lang, is a very detailed model with toes, toe pads and eyes. I experimented with printer paper, then used green tissue foil(30*30cm).

Easter Rabbit

This is the rabbit designed by Jun Maekawa. I used 15*15cm kami paper to fold this model.

Coromarant

This coromarant is designed by Michael LaFosse. Diagrams can be found in the book "Origami Art". I used normal printer paper for this model.

Scaled Fish

This is the scaled fish designed by JoNakashima based on Davorvinko's fish. I used a printer paper cut into a rectangle to make to make this model.

Dog

This is the sheep dog designed by Seth Friedman. You can find an instructional video on his youtube channel along with few other beautiful models. I used a normal A4 printer paper but a larger sheet is highly recommended.

Fox

This model is designed by Hoang Tien Quyet. I used a square sheet of paper with the side length of 30 cm. I used a coffee tissue foil but wet folding with water color paper will have better results.

Rat

This is the rat designed by Eric Joisel. I used blue copy paper for this model but really most papers work for this model.

Humpback Whale

This humpback whale is designed by Michael Lafosse. Diagrams can be found in the book "Origami Arts". I used a 15*15 cm kami paper.

Rabbit

This is the rabbit designed by Hsi-Min Tai. This is the complex model and I used 15*15 cm kami paper.
