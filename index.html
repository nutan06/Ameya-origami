A brief history of origami...

The exact origin of origami art is not known.

China invented the paper and Buddhist monks brought it to Japan. Japan developed origami to a very high art form.

Most Origami instructions were passed on by oral tradition. The oldest known document about Japanese origami, the Senbazuru Orikata, surfaced in 1797.

While origami is often considered a children's pastime in west, its long history in Japan has enmeshed it in the cultural identity.

Rhino

This model is designed by Quentin Trollip and can be found in the book "Origami Sequence". I used 35*35 cm grey leather paper for this model.

Fiery Dragon

This is one of the very first complex models I folded. Kade Chan used various methods that created exquisite details in the finished model,like the tail spikes,eyes and horns. A sheet of 30*30 cm tissue works very good with this model.

Banana slug

This model is designed by Micheal LaFosse and can be found in the book "Origami Art".I used 15*15cm kami paper for this model.
